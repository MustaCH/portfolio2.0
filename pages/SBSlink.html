Project Description

As simple as necessary, in this case I have created a site to buy tickets for a start up of alternative music events. The premise is simple, the customer accesses the link to purchase their tickets, completes their data and implementing the API MercadoPago Checkout PRO the customer can make the payment for your ticket and once processed the same will be redirected back to the site where you will confirm the transaction and will proceed to send your ticket via e-mail.
